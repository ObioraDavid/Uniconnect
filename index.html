Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about UniConnect and how it can help improve your university experience.

How do I submit a new support request?
‚Üì

Submitting a request is simple! After logging in, click the "New Request" button on your dashboard. Select the appropriate category, fill in the details of your request, attach any relevant files, and submit. You'll receive a confirmation and tracking number instantly.

Which university departments are available on UniConnect?
‚Üì

UniConnect integrates with all major university departments including Academic Affairs, Financial Aid, Student Housing, IT Services, Campus Security, Library Services, Career Services, and Health & Wellness. More departments are being added regularly based on student feedback.

How quickly can I expect a response to my request?
‚Üì

Response times vary by department and request urgency, but our system prioritizes all submissions. Most requests receive an initial response within 24 hours. You can check the status of your request anytime in the app, and you'll receive real-time notifications as updates occur.

Is my personal information secure on UniConnect?
‚Üì

Absolutely! We take data security very seriously. UniConnect uses industry-standard encryption protocols, and all information is stored securely according to university privacy policies. Your personal information is only accessible to authorized personnel directly involved in resolving your requests.

Can I use UniConnect on my mobile device?
‚Üì

Yes! UniConnect is fully mobile-responsive and works on any device with a web browser. We also offer dedicated iOS and Android apps for an enhanced mobile experience with push notifications, offline capabilities, and quick access features.
